Put your background.mp4 file in this folder (assets/background.mp4).

Recommended: under 15MB, compressed (H.264, 1080p is plenty — no need for
higher resolution since it's just a background). If you don't add a video,
the app still works fine — it just shows the plain dark/light background
color instead.

---

Also put a logo.png here (assets/logo.png) for the splash screen shown on
first load and after logout. Square image works best (e.g. 512x512).
If you don't add one, the splash just shows the app name/text — no crash.
